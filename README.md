## 🚀 About Me

Hello! I'm **Jojo**, a final year student and professional **UI/UX Designer & Developer**. I have a deep passion for building digital solutions that are not only high-performing but also visually stunning and accessible.

Lately, I’ve been focusing on **Flutter** for cross-platform mobile apps and **Figma** for creating gamified design systems. I enjoy bridging the gap between design and code, ensuring every pixel has a purpose.

What's unique about me? I'm most productive at night—when it's quiet, I can truly immerse myself in the world of code. Coding in the middle of the night gives me a sense of calm and creative flow ✨
